Around the market

What You'll Find Under the Canopy

Six thousand square feet of the good stuff — produce trucked in fresh five times a week, flowers raised in our own greenhouses, and a farm store full of old-fashioned finds.

Farm-Fresh Produce

Sweet corn, tomatoes, melons, peaches and plums — some of our growers have supplied us for 45+ years.

Greenhouse & Flowers

Hanging baskets, bedding plants and vegetable starts — the spring tradition of half of northeast Indiana.

The Farm Store

Amish cheese, fresh butter, bacon, bulk candy, jams, spices — and cookies and cinnamon rolls people drive out for.

Fall Harvest

Mums in every color, pumpkins, gourds, corn stalks and Indian corn until we close for winter.

Since 1937

It Started With a Bushel of Potatoes

In 1937, Joella Decker set a basket of potatoes at the edge of the road in Huntertown with a hand-painted sign, just to see if anyone would stop. They did. Nearly ninety years, five generations of Decker women, two fires and one tornado later — we're still here, and still family-run.
